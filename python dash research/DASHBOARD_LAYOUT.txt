Charts:
  • Donut: Multi-color (status categories)
  • Funnel: Blue gradient (progression)
  • Bar: Green gradient (positive trend)
  • Line: Blue line with markers
  • Bubble: Viridis scale (value indication)
  • Stacked: Status-specific colors
