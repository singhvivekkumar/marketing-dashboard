📈 CHART DESCRIPTIONS

1️⃣ DONUT CHART — Lead Status
   Shows: Distribution of leads by status (Won, Lost, Pending, etc.)
   Why: Quick overview of overall lead health
   Use: Identify majority status and opportunities
   
2️⃣ FUNNEL CHART — Pipeline Stages
   Shows: Number of leads in each stage
   Why: Visualize sales progression
   Use: Spot bottlenecks in pipeline

3️⃣ HORIZONTAL BAR — Top 10 Customers
   Shows: Customers ranked by value (₹ Lakhs)
   Why: Identify key revenue sources
   Use: Focus on high-value customers

4️⃣ LINE CHART — Revenue Forecast
   Shows: Monthly revenue trend + forecast
   Why: Plan future revenue
   Use: Financial planning and predictions

5️⃣ BUBBLE CHART — Owner Performance
   Shows: Each owner's leads, wins, and value
   Why: Measure individual productivity
   Use: Recognize top performers, identify support needs
   
   Details:
   • X-axis = Total Leads handled
   • Y-axis = Won Deals count
   • Bubble Size = Total Value (₹ Lakhs)

6️⃣ STACKED BAR — Business Unit Breakdown
   Shows: Lead status distribution per SBU
   Why: Compare SBU performance
   Use: Identify successful units and areas for improvement

📞 TROUBLESHOOTING

Issue: "No data showing"
Solution: 
  1. Verify Excel files in src/Leads_Data/
  2. Check column names match exactly
  3. Restart dashboard: python app.py

Issue: "Port 8050 already in use"
Solution:
  Edit .env and change:
  DASH_PORT=8051

Issue: Charts not updating when filtering
Solution:
  • Clear browser cache (Ctrl+F5)
  • Restart dashboard
  • Check browser console (F12)
