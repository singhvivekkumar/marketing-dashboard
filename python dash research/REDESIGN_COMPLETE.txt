📊 THE 6 MAIN CHARTS AT A GLANCE

1. DONUT CHART - Lead Status
   ✓ Shows: Won (50%) | Pending (30%) | Lost (15%) | Others (5%)
   ✓ Why: Quick overview of overall lead health
   ✓ Action: Identifies status with most leads

2. FUNNEL CHART - Pipeline Stages
   ✓ Shows: Planned (40) → Achieved (25) → Closed (5)
   ✓ Why: Visualize sales progression
   ✓ Action: Find stage bottlenecks

3. HORIZONTAL BAR - Top 10 Customers
   ✓ Shows: Customer A (₹500L) → Customer J (₹100L)
   ✓ Why: Focus on high-value relationships
   ✓ Action: Prioritize top customers

4. LINE CHART - Revenue Forecast
   ✓ Shows: Monthly trend + forecast line
   ✓ Why: Plan future revenue
   ✓ Action: Financial planning & predictions

5. BUBBLE CHART - Owner Performance
   ✓ Shows: Each owner's leads (X) vs wins (Y) vs value (bubble size)
   ✓ Why: Measure individual productivity
   ✓ Action: Recognize top performers

6. STACKED BAR - Business Unit Breakdown
   ✓ Shows: Each SBU split by status (Won/Pending/Lost)
   ✓ Why: Compare unit performance
   ✓ Action: Identify successful units
